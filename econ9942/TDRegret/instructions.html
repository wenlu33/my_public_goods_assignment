Instructions for Pilot Experiment

Welcome to the ExCEN laboratory and thanks for participating in this experiment!

You are about to participate in a session on decision-making, and you will be paid for your participation in cash at the end of the session. You will be paid in private and have no obligation to tell others how much you earned. At the end of the experiment, the money (expressed in $ED) that you have earned will be converted in USD at the exchange rate of $ED100 = 1 USD. You will receive at least $5 USD for your participation today. In addition, you can earn up to $30 USD depending on the actions each round.

You will have to answer all review questions about this instruction to continue with the experiment. Please read carefully, if at any time you have any questions, raise your hand and experimenter will come to you to answer it.
